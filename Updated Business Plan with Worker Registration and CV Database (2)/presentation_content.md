## Slide 2: The Problem & Our Solution
### Title: The Challenge: Unverified & Inefficient Workforce Supply
### Key Points:
*   **Problem:** High risk of unverified workers, age fraud, and lack of accountability in the traditional supply chain.
*   **Problem:** Inefficient recruitment, especially for workers traveling from rural areas to Addis Ababa.
*   **Solution:** HomeLink Addis provides a **secure, centralized, and fully verified platform** connecting workers with Family Employers and Agencies.
*   **Solution:** We offer a **1-Month Replacement Guarantee** to build client trust and reduce hiring risk.

## Slide 3: Core Business Model & Revenue
### Title: A Scalable Model: Two Primary Customer Segments
### Key Points:
*   **Worker Supply:** Recruit, register, house, and manage a pool of vetted domestic workers.
*   **Segment 1: Addis Family Employers:** Direct placement for domestic work in Addis Ababa homes.
*   **Segment 2: Local & Foreign Agencies:** Reliable source of pre-vetted candidates for the international market (B2B).
*   **Revenue Model:** Worker **Subscription Fees** for registration and access to the talent pool.

## Slide 4: **NEW** Feature: Multi-Channel Registration (Accessibility)
### Title: Reaching the Workforce: Registration via Messaging Platforms
### Key Points:
*   **Goal:** Maximize reach to workers in rural areas with limited internet access.
*   **Channels:** Workers can initiate registration via **WhatsApp, TikTok, Telegram, and IMO**.
*   **Process:** Worker sends National ID photo to a dedicated account to start the process.
*   **Benefit:** Low-barrier entry ensures a larger, more diverse talent pool.

## Slide 5: **NEW** Feature: Mandatory Verification (Security & Compliance)
### Title: Zero Tolerance: Mandatory Age and Contact Verification
### Key Points:
*   **Age Compliance:** Automated OCR scan of National ID ensures all workers are **18 years or older**. Registration is immediately halted if underaged.
*   **Emergency Contact Security:** Worker must provide the **National ID of their emergency contact**.
*   **Origin Verification:** System verifies the emergency contact's ID is from the worker's **original ruler area** before travel to Addis.
*   **Impact:** Ensures legal compliance and provides a reliable, verified point of contact in the worker's home community.

## Slide 6: **NEW** Feature: Auto-CV & Database (Efficiency)
### Title: Professionalizing the Workforce: Automated CV Generation
### Key Points:
*   **Auto-CV Generation:** System automatically compiles all verified data (Skills, Experience, Training Badges) into a professional, standardized CV (PDF).
*   **Centralized Database:** A single source of truth for all worker profiles.
*   **Resource Sharing:** CVs are securely shared with authorized Family Employers and Agencies via their respective portals.
*   **Security:** No direct contact information is revealed until a formal hiring process is initiated.

## Slide 7: Worker Portal & Pipeline
### Title: Worker Management: From Registration to Placement
### Key Points:
*   **Registration Data:** Captures Bio-Data, National ID (Kebele ID), Skill Self-Assessment, and Job Preferences.
*   **Status Dashboard:** Workers track their application status: *Pending Review, Approved & Available, Hired.*
*   **Training & Vetting:** Workers must complete orientation and training modules (e.g., House Safety, Etiquette) before approval.
*   **Accommodation Center:** Provides temporary housing and check-in/check-out logging.

## Slide 8: Family Employer Portal
### Title: The Domestic Market: Easy & Trustworthy Hiring
### Key Points:
*   **Vetting:** Light verification of employers (ID, Address).
*   **Search & Filter:** Employers search the database by Skills, Age Range, and Experience Level.
*   **Hiring Workflow:** "Request Interview" and "Hire This Worker" buttons streamline the process.
*   **Guarantee:** Clear display of the **1-Month Replacement Guarantee** on the contract.

## Slide 9: Agency Portal (B2B)
### Title: The International Market: Bulk Supply & Advanced Tools
### Key Points:
*   **Stricter Vetting:** Agencies require business license and MOLSA verification.
*   **Advanced Search:** Filters for international placement (e.g., "Passport Holder," "Willing to work abroad").
*   **Bulk Operations:** Agencies can Shortlist candidates and use the **Bulk Export/CV Download** function.
*   **Efficiency:** Provides a reliable, pre-vetted supply chain for international placement.

## Slide 10: Operational Flow & Verification Points
### Title: The HomeLink Process: A Secure Data Flow
### Key Points:
*   **Worker Registers:** Data captured via multi-channel intake.
*   **Verification Gate:** **Age Check (18+)** and **Emergency Contact Origin Match** are mandatory.
*   **Approval:** HomeLink staff manually vet and approve the profile. Auto-CV is generated.
*   **Placement:** Worker is placed with a Family Employer or Agency.
*   **Post-Placement:** Replacement Guarantee timer starts; feedback loop updates the worker's Trust Score.
